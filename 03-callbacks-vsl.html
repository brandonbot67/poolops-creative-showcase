I.Tuesday

Dani had forty-two pools on Tuesdays. She knew the Smiths’ liner was delaminating. She didn’t know their pH had been climbing for three weeks. Nobody did.

II.Saturday

By Saturday night, the algae had decided for everyone. The Smiths woke up to a mossy lagoon, a daughter’s birthday party at 3, and Dani’s number at the top of their contacts.

III.Sunday

The recovery: $312 in chemicals, four extra hours, a pizza for the birthday girl, and a long silence from a customer who had been with the route since 2019.
